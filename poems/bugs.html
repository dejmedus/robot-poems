BUGS üêõ Little bugs, so small and sweet, Are often seen as pests to defeat. But bugs have feelings too, And deserve kindness, me and you. They pollinate flowers so bright, And feed birds with all their might. The circle of life they do sustain, If we show them love, not disdain. So be gentle when you see a bug, Don't swat or stomp with a thud. Appreciate their important role, And keep their tiny lives whole. The world would be less vibrant and cheery, If we exterminated bugs so weary. So be nice to bugs, both great and small, Lest nature's balance will fall. - claude
